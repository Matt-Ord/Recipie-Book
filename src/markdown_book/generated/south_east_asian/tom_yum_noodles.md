# Tom Yum Noodle Soup

* **Cook time:** 15 mins
* **Prep time:** 10 mins
* **Serves** 2 people 

---

A hearty, "one-bowl" version of the classic Thai hot and sour soup. By adding rice noodles, minced protein, and crunchy toppings, the traditional starter is transformed into a filling and vibrant main course.

## Ingredients

### The Broth Base

* **2 tbsp** Tom yum paste
* **1 litre** Chicken or vegetable stock
* **2 stalks** Lemongrass (bruised and cut into sections, optional)
* **4-5** Kaffir lime leaves (torn, optional)
* **1 small piece** Galangal or ginger (sliced)

### The Noodles & Bulk

* **200g** Rice noodles (medium "Pad Thai" style or thin vermicelli)
* **200g** Raw shrimp (peeled) or sliced chicken breast
* **100g** Minced pork or chicken
* **150g** Mushrooms (oyster, straw, or button)
* **1 handful** Bean sprouts

### Seasoning (To Taste)

* **2 tbsp** Fish sauce
* **1-2 tbsp** Lime juice
* **1 tsp** Sugar (palm or brown)
* **Optional:** Thai bird's eye chilies (crushed)

### Toppings (Optional)

* **1 tbsp** Roasted peanuts (crushed)
* **1 small bunch** Fresh cilantro (chopped)
* **1 stalk** Spring onion (sliced)

---

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the Noodles:** Soak the rice noodles in warm water according to the package instructions (usually 15-20 minutes) until flexible. Drain and set aside.
2. **Infuse the Broth:** In a large pot, bring the stock to a boil. Add the lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, and galangal. Let it simmer for 5 minutes until the broth becomes fragrant.
3. **Add the Paste:** Stir in the Tom yum paste until fully dissolved. Add the mushrooms and minced protein, stirring to break up the meat into small crumbles.
4. **Cook the Seafood/Protein:** Add the shrimp or sliced chicken. Simmer for 2-3 minutes until the protein is just cooked through.
5. **Final Seasoning:** Turn off the heat. Stir in the fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice. **Taste the broth**â€”it should be a balanced hit of spicy, sour, and salty.
6. **Quick Blanch:** Quickly boil the soaked noodles and bean sprouts in a separate pot of water for 1-2 minutes. Drain and divide into serving bowls.
7. **Assemble:** Ladle the hot soup and proteins over the noodles.
8. **Garnish:** Top each bowl with crushed peanuts, cilantro, and spring onions. Serve immediately while piping hot.
