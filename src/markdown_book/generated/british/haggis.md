# Haggis

Prep Time: 30 min

Cook Time: 60 min

Serves: 6
## Ingredients
* 1200 grams potato
* 1 turnip
* milk
* haggis
* sausages
* gravy
* peas to serve
* carrots to serve


## Method
* Peel and roughly chop the potatoes and turnip, boil. Once soft drain the potatoes and mash along with milk, butter, pepper to taste.
* Cook the haggis and sausages and serve.
